About US

Depression And Trauma Therapy provides specialized depression therapy services and trauma focused treatment designed to support long-term emotional healing. Our licensed trauma therapists offer personalized care for PTSD, anxiety, and mood disorders through EMDR trauma sessions, professional trauma counseling, and structured recovery plans.

Whether you're navigating complex trauma or seeking compassionate mental health support, our approach blends evidence-based methods with a warm, client-centered experience. With both in-person and virtual therapy options, we help individuals regain balance, confidence, and clarity. EMDR (Eye Movement Desensitization and Reprocessing) is an evidence-based therapy that helps process traumatic memories and reduce distress. During EMDR sessions, our licensed trauma therapists guide you through bilateral stimulation while you recall difficult memories, allowing your brain to reprocess them in a healthier way. It's particularly effective for PTSD, complex trauma, anxiety, and emotional stress.
